Key Features

Key Features

The Robot Car with Flame, Buzzer, RGB Light, LCD Monitor, Sound Sensor, and DHT Sensor is an advanced and versatile robotic vehicle designed for various applications. This intelligent car combines multiple sensors and functionalities to provide a comprehensive and interactive solution.


Flame Sensor:

The built-in flame sensor enables the robot car to detect the presence of flames or fire. When a flame is detected, the car triggers the buzzer, providing an audible alert to draw attention to potential fire hazards.


Buzzer:

The integrated buzzer provides audio feedback and alerts. It is programmed to activate when the flame sensor detects flames, ensuring immediate notification and enhancing safety measures.


RGB Light:

The car is equipped with an RGB light, which can emit a wide range of colors. The light is controlled by the sound sensor and is programmed to turn on when the sensor detects sound. This feature adds a visual element to the car's interactivity and provides a vibrant display when sound is detected.


LCD Monitor:

The car is equipped with an LCD monitor to display real-time information. The DHT sensor, which measures temperature and humidity, flashes the current temperature on the LCD monitor. This feature enables easy monitoring of environmental conditions while the car is in operation.


Sound Sensor:

The car's sound sensor detects sound levels in the environment. When a sound is detected, it triggers the RGB light to turn on, providing a visual indication of the detected sound. This feature enhances the car's interactivity and responsiveness to its surroundings.


DHT Sensor:

The inclusion of a DHT (Digital Humidity and Temperature) sensor allows real-time monitoring of ambient temperature and humidity levels. The car utilizes this sensor to flash the current temperature on the LCD monitor, providing instant temperature information at a glance.
